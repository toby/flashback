Example recipes

Five fast entry points into the 2000 corpus recipes.

Recipe 01

Portal utility glow

Use for: dashboards, search products, account systems.

Palette
ice blue, white, navy, alert orange.
Layout
portal blocks, search bars, utility rails.
Motion
load, expand, search, refresh.

Risk: retro web kitsch.
Accuracy: keep the task focus strong.

 Recipe 02

Translucent device optimism

Use for: hardware launches, accessories, onboarding surfaces.

Palette
aqua, milk white, lime, silver.
Layout
object-first compositions, floating callouts.
Motion
glow, dock, snap, wake, charge.

Risk: generic Y2K slime aesthetics.
Accuracy: keep industrial precision visible.

 Recipe 03

Nokia text-social

Use for: messaging tools, notification systems, compact social features.

Palette
graphite, screen green, pale silver, amber.
Layout
compact modules, message stacks, keyed navigation.
Motion
type, send, vibrate, scroll, store.

Risk: generic phone nostalgia.
Accuracy: preserve one-thumb constraint.

 Recipe 04

Boutique minimal service

Use for: hospitality brands, premium commerce, architecture-led products.

Palette
warm white, stone, glass grey, pale wood.
Layout
wide margins, clear pathways, image breathing room.
Motion
fade, slide, open, welcome.

Risk: generic luxury minimalism.
Accuracy: keep turn-of-millennium service cues.

 Recipe 05

Millennium chrome current

Use for: entertainment, sports, promo microsites, motion campaigns.

Palette
chrome, electric blue, black, white flare.
Layout
diagonal sweeps, metallic frames, reflective panels.
Motion
sweep, rotate, shimmer, accelerate.

Risk: parody chrome overload.
Accuracy: keep the composition tight.
